Ramen noodle soup

This ramen sure looks nice!!!

Ramen is a common dish you can see on almost every anime. It established itself as a dish almost every kid wants to try, and I'm sure you too are kinda interested.

Ramen consists of noodles, some eggs and vegetables. This is an easy one, so grab your sticks because in exactly 15 minutes you will be eating your first homemade ramen.

Ingredients:

  • 3½ cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat;stir to break up noodles.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and gingeer.
  4. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
